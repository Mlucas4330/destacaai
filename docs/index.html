Privacy Policy

DestacaAI - Last updated: April 2026

Overview

DestacaAI does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

Data Collected

The following data is stored locally on your device to operate the extension:

  • Your uploaded CV file (PDF)
  • Saved job descriptions
  • Generated CV content
  • Your LLM provider API key
  • Extension configuration and preferences

Data Storage

Data is stored using chrome.storage.local and IndexedDB. No external database or cloud storage is used. Uninstalling the extension permanently deletes all stored data.

Third-Party Services

When generating a CV, your CV text and the job description are sent directly from your browser to your chosen LLM provider. DestacaAI does not intercept or log this data.

  • OpenAI — Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic — Privacy Policy
  • Google — Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Saved jobs and generated CVs are automatically deleted after 10 days. You can manually delete all data at any time via the Settings screen.
